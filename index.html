Research Overview

Our research focuses on understanding and combating health misinformation in the digital age. We examine how misinformation spreads across social media platforms and develop evidence-based strategies for correction.

Key Research Areas

Social Media and Health Misinformation

Investigating how health misinformation spreads through social networks and identifying effective intervention strategies.

Correction Strategies

Developing and testing evidence-based approaches to correct health misinformation and promote accurate health information.

Digital Health Literacy

Exploring how individuals evaluate health information online and factors that influence their assessment of health claims.
